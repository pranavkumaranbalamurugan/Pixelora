About Symposium

A Legacy of Innovation

PIXELORA 2K26 is a flagship symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science Engineering, bringing together innovation, creativity, and technical excellence.


Built on the vision of "A NEW BIRTH," it provides a platform for students to explore, showcase, and innovate. Join us in redefining the boundaries of the digital world.
